about me

Luisa Kahlfeldt is a product and furniture designer. Originally from Berlin, she has studied at Central Saint Martins in London where she graduated with a First Class (Hons) Bachelor in Industrial Design. After graduating in 2014 she was awarded a Designer Residence scholarship at the Bauhaus Foundation in Dessau. Since then she has gained practical experience working as a designer for Tom Dixon and Barber Osgerby.
